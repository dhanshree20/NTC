Waterproofing

Conventional Brick Bat Coba For Toilet Block

Brick bat coba is a traditional waterproofing method used for toilet blocks to prevent leakage and maintain structural integrity.This method provides a reliable and economical solution for waterproofing in toilet blocks, though it may require maintenance over time.

Common Methods :
1) Surface Preparation: Clean the base and apply a waterproofing slurry.
2)Brick Bat Layering: Lay broken bricks in cement mortar at required slopes.
3)Finishing: Cover with a slurry coat or screed for a smooth, waterproof finish.
