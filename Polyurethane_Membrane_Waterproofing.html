Polyurethane Membrane Waterproofing

Polyurethane membrane waterproofing is a highly effective and flexible solution used to protect surfaces from water ingress, providing long-lasting waterproofing for both new and old structures. Polyurethane membrane waterproofing is a premium solution for a wide range of applications, offering flexible, durable, and seamless protection against water ingress.

Common Materials :
1)Pure Polyurethane: Excellent performance in high-traffic and exposed areas.
2)Modified Polyurethane: Improved chemical resistance, ideal for industrial applications.

Features

  • Flexibility: Expands and contracts with the building, accommodating movement without cracking.
  • Durability: Resistant to UV rays, extreme temperatures, and weather conditions.
  • Seamless Application: Forms a continuous, joint-free membrane, eliminating leak points.
  • High Adhesion: Strong bonding to various surfaces like concrete, metal, and wood.
  • Chemical Resistance: Protects against chemicals, oils, and other harsh substances.

Applications

  • Roofs and Terraces: Ideal for flat roofs, terraces, and balconies.
  • Basements and Foundations: Prevents moisture penetration in underground spaces.
  • Swimming Pools: Provides waterproofing and flexibility in pool areas.
  • Parking Areas: Protects parking decks and ramps from water damage.
