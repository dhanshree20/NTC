Anti-Static(ESD) Flooring

Anti-static or Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) flooring is designed to prevent the buildup of static electricity and safely dissipate electrical charges. It is essential in environments where static can damage sensitive equipment or pose safety risks.This flooring is critical for maintaining operational safety, protecting sensitive equipment, and ensuring regulatory compliance.

Common Materials:
1) Epoxy and Polyurethane: Coatings with conductive additives for static dissipation.
2) Vinyl : Sheets or tiles with embedded conductive layers.
3) Carpet Tiles: Made with conductive fibers for office environments.
