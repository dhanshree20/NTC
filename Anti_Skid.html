Anti-Skid Flooring

Anti-skid flooring is designed to enhance safety by providing slip resistance, particularly in environments prone to wet or slippery conditions. It reduces the risk of accidents and ensures compliance with safety standards.Anti-skid flooring is an essential solution for improving safety and minimizing slip-and-fall accidents in diverse settings.

Common Materials:
1) Epoxy with Aggregate: Embedded sand or quartz for enhanced traction.
2) Rubber Flooring: Naturally slip-resistant and flexible.
3) Vinyl or PVC: Textured or coated for slip prevention.
4) Anti-Slip Mats or Coatings: Applied to existing floors for added safety.
