Waterproofing

Conventional Brick Bat Coba For Terraces and Balconies

Brick bat coba is a traditional waterproofing technique used on terraces and balconies to prevent water leakage, ensuring the longevity and safety of the structure. Brick bat coba remains a traditional and practical solution for terrace and balcony waterproofing, although it requires periodic maintenance for optimal performance.

Common Methods :
1) Surface Preparation: Clean and smoothen the terrace or balcony surface.
2)Brick Bat Layering: Spread broken bricks in a cement mortar mix to create a sloped layer for drainage.
3)Finishing: Apply a screed or slurry coat to smoothen the surface and enhance waterproofing.
